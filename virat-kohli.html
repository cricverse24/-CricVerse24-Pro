⭐ Virat Kohli Career

Virat Kohli duniya ke behtareen batsmen mein shumar kiye jate hain. Unhon ne Test, ODI aur T20 cricket mein bohot se records apne naam kiye hain.

Virat Kohli ne apni fitness, consistency aur aggressive batting ki wajah se cricket ki duniya mein ek alag maqam hasil kiya. Unki cover drive aur chase innings fans ko bohot pasand hain.

Unhon ne India ko kai aham matches jitwaye aur kai ICC tournaments mein shandaar performances di hain. ODI cricket mein unki centuries aur run-scoring speed unhein modern era ke greatest batsmen mein shamil karti hai.

Virat Kohli aaj bhi cricket ke sab se mashhoor players mein se ek hain aur har match mein naye records banane ki salahiyat rakhte hain.
