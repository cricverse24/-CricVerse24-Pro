⭐ Shahid Afridi Career

Shahid Afridi Pakistan cricket ke tareekh ke sab se mashhoor all-rounders mein se ek hain. Unhon ne apni dhamaakedar batting, leg-spin bowling aur shandaar fielding se duniya bhar ke fans ke dil jeete.

Afridi ne apne career ke aaghaz mein hi record tod century bana kar duniya ko hairan kar diya. Isi wajah se unhein "Boom Boom Afridi" ke naam se jana jata hai.

Unhon ne Pakistan ko kai yaadgar matches jitwaye aur cricket ki duniya mein apni alag pehchan banayi. Aaj bhi lakhon fans unhein Pakistan ke azeem cricketers mein shumar karte hain.
